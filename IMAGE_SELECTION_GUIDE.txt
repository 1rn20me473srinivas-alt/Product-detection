# Quick Reference: What Images to Look For

## Product 1: Dark Chocolate Bar
- Wrapped chocolate bar showing foil/paper wrapper
- Close-up showing "85%" or cocoa percentage text
- Side/corner view showing thickness and ridges

## Product 2: Yoga Mat
- Rolled mat with carrying strap visible
- Flat mat showing texture/pattern
- Edge view showing thickness

## Product 3: Fitness Water Bottle
- Transparent plastic bottle (can see through it)
- Time markers or measurements visible on side
- Cap/spout detail

## Product 4: Smartwatch
- Screen ON with display visible (NOT just black screen)
- Watch on wrist showing band
- Side view showing buttons/crown

## Product 5: Wireless Earbuds
- Charging case open showing earbuds inside
- Earbuds outside case showing round tips
- Close-up of case showing glossy finish

## Product 6: Power Bank
- Front showing LED display/battery indicator
- Side showing USB-C ports
- Metallic/aluminum finish visible

## Product 7: Glasses
- Front view showing transparent lenses
- Folded showing temple arms/hinges
- Side profile showing arms extended

## Product 8: Lunch Box
- OPEN lid showing divided compartments (IMPORTANT!)
- Closed box from top
- Side view showing sections

## Product 9: Stainless Steel Water Bottle
- Full bottle showing brushed metal surface
- Cap/lid detail (circular top)
- NO transparency (solid metal, not see-through)

## Product 10: Smartphone
- Screen ON with bright display/UI visible (CRITICAL!)
- Back view showing camera circles
- Side view showing thin profile

## Product 11: Deodorant Spray
- Full can showing brand/label
- Top view showing spray nozzle/cap
- Cylindrical shape clearly visible

---

## IMPORTANT DISTINCTIONS:

### Water Bottles:
- Product 3 (Fitness) = TRANSPARENT plastic, can see liquid inside
- Product 9 (Steel) = SOLID metal, completely opaque

### Screens:
- Product 4 (Smartwatch) = Small square screen on wrist
- Product 10 (Smartphone) = Large rectangular screen in hand

### Features to Prioritize:
1. Unique identifiers (screens, compartments, transparency)
2. Different angles (front, side, top)
3. Clear lighting (bright, not dark/shadowy)
4. High resolution (avoid tiny thumbnails)
